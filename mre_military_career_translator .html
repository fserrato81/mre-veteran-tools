Mission Ready Education career exploration tool for translating military experience into civilian occupations, transferable skills, education pathways, and resume keywords.

Quick Review

Results will appear here

Select a military career field and click Translate Experience. This tool is best used as a live webinar conversation starter or one-on-one advising aid.

How to Use During Webinars

  1. Ask the veteran for their MOS, rating, AFSC, or closest career field.
  2. Select years of experience and leadership level.
  3. Click Translate Experience.
  4. Use the results to discuss civilian job titles, resume language, and possible education paths.
  5. Remind attendees this is a career exploration tool, not a guarantee of employment or credential approval.
Disclaimer: This tool provides general career exploration guidance. Civilian job matches, education requirements, and licensing needs vary by employer, state, industry, and individual background. Veterans should verify requirements with employers, schools, credentialing agencies, VA, and workforce professionals.
